About

Welcome to my portfolio project!. My name is Sylvia Ani, an aspiring software engineer and a student of Holberton School.I created this Calendar TO-DO App, with the aim to make people become more productive with themselves daily. As someone, who likes to procrastinate a lot, I used myself as a source of inspiration to design this project. The app is designed to allow user schedule their daily tasks and even future projects. I created this app on February, 2024 and of course as a courtesy to the Holberton School and ALX.
