ABOUT ME

Sylvia Ani

I am an aspiring software engineer and a student of Holberton School. I created this Calendar TO-DO App, with the aim of making people become more productive with themselves daily and also utilizing their time effectively. As someone who procrastinates a lot, I used myself as a source of inspiration to design this project. I also gathered data in trying to estimate how my app would make an effect globally by speaking to people. I finally came to a conclusion that people find it strenous to manage their 24 hours, which of course is where my app comes in.
The app is designed to allow users schedule their daily tasks and even for future projects. I created this app on February, 2024 and of course as a courtesy to the Holberton School and ALX.
The app was designed using HTML, CSS and Javascript, using Node.JS and Express.JS for it's backend. I single-handedly designed this project from scratch, fitting it for all audience and age-groups. It is easy to use, navigate and user-friendly.
